Understanding proteome diversity through RNA

I study the roles of RNA processing and ribosome composition in changing cellular outcomes with a focus on heart disease and cancer. I use a combination of high-throughput and targeted approaches in both murine models and cell culture systems to uncover unexplored mechanisms of gene expression regulation.
